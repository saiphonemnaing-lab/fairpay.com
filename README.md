# fairpay.com
FairPay is a website built during HackDavis to voice unfair pays, and in addition, helps students and people switching jobs to have salaries and wages data. It is a common ground for community folks of different racial backgrounds and genders to advocate for parity in compensation. There are 3 mains of the project.
  1. Login with Google
       With simplicity focused, we only build a Google login at the moment. This will enhance users' data.
   2. Two Sub Pages: Stories & Pages
        We have divided our main page into two sub pages: Stories and Pages. We envision people to share their story about recruiment, road to landing internship, experience about switching careers, tips and tricks for promotion, or importantly, injustice acts they witnesses in jobs. By this, we aim to advocate to change by the users to voice their demands for better work environment.
  3. Data
       Verification: We want to give users a summary statistics of each job roles' pay. And in order for us to provide information to users, we need valid users input. So, we use LinkedIn to verify users validity.
       Statistics: Our idea is to provide statistics of Mean, Median, and Middle 50%. With more input from different valid users, we will have a normal distribution of each pay. Then, more data will allow us, with the help of statistics to show if an input is an outlier.
